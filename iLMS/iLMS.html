i-LMS

(Intelligent Learning Management System)

About the Project

This project is based on the idea of making education personalized for the students. In order words, based on student's understanding of the concepts and performance in the assessments, further course content and activities will be provided to them.

The implementation of i-LMS is divided into 5 separate modules each implemented by a team of 6 members, namely,

  • User/Learner analystics
  • Program management
  • Course personalisation
  • Content management
  • Assessment system

The target is to make a better and robust version of lms which personalises education using React to replace current Angular based lms, NgLMS for MSIT program.

Development Process

The development methodology used for the project was scrum. Each team performed 8 sprints, each of duration one-week with sprint review taking place evert Saturday. Also, a pseudo Product Owner was appointed from every team to keep a track of Product Backlog.

My Role

As a part of the Assessment System team, I was involved in development of a user-interface for the students to take assessment which will be auto-evaluated and an assessment builder which eases the creation of assessments for instructors and mentors, since, in current version, i.e., NgLMS, we have a separate backend team which uses manually written json files for the same.

I also played the role of pseudo Product Owner for my team and kept a track of Product backlog which consisted of the features and user-stories to be implemented.
